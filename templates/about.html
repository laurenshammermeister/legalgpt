About LegalGPT

What is LegalGPT?

LegalGPT is an AI-powered web application designed to help people understand complex legal language. Our tool translates legal jargon from contracts, terms of service, privacy policies, and other legal documents into simple, comprehensible explanations.

How It Works

1. Paste Legal Text

Copy and paste the legal clause or document you need help understanding.

2. AI Analysis

Our AI processes the text and interprets the legal language.

3. Get Results

Receive a clear explanation and risk assessment in seconds.

Key Features

  • Plain Language Explanations: Convert complex legal jargon into everyday language
  • Risk Identification: Highlight potential legal risks in terms and agreements
  • Audience Selection: Choose between layperson explanations or technical legal analysis
  • Concern Level Rating: Understand how risky a clause might be with our 1-5 rating system

Important Disclaimer

LegalGPT is an educational tool designed to help understand legal language, but it is not a substitute for professional legal advice. The explanations and risk assessments provided are generated by AI and should be used for informational purposes only. For legal matters, always consult with a qualified attorney.
